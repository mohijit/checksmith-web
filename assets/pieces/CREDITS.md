# Chess piece artwork

Burnett, from Wikimedia Commons:
<https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:SVG_chess_pieces>

License: [CC BY-SA 3.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/).
Unmodified — only renamed (from `Chess_{piece}{l|d}t45.svg` to
`{piece}-{white|black}.svg`) for readability. Attribution is also shown live
on the page footer.
